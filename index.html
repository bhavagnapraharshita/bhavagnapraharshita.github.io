🌊 SA Coastal Flood Risk Viewer
New
Climate Data Analysis · Coastal Hazards · Python · DEW Portfolio Project

An end-to-end coastal hazard analysis pipeline integrating sea level rise projections with South Australia's coastline to deliver data-driven flood risk insights across three climate scenarios. Built to demonstrate skills directly relevant to the Climate Data Analyst – Coastal Hazards role at the Department for Environment and Water (DEW).

Python GeoPandas Folium Matplotlib GDA2020 / EPSG:7854 SSP2-4.5 SSP5-8.5 CSIRO SLR Projections GeoJSON Interactive Dashboard
📊 Key findings: 3,061 km² at risk by 2050 (0.5m SLR) → 12,238 km² at risk by 2100 (2.0m SLR, SSP5-8.5) — a 4× increase under high-emissions pathway

The pipeline includes automated data ingestion and QA, proxy flood extent modelling across three SLR scenarios, weighted risk scoring (Low → Extreme), an interactive Folium web dashboard with scenario toggles and clickable popups, publication-quality charts, and a methodology report with data quality statement.

🌿 Forest Carbon Stock Monitoring Dashboard
In Progress
ArcGIS Online · Sentinel-2 · GEDI LiDAR · Mount Lofty Ranges

An end-to-end geospatial dashboard integrating Sentinel-2 multispectral imagery, GEDI LiDAR data, and ArcGIS Online to monitor and visualise forest carbon stocks in the Mount Lofty Ranges, South Australia.

Sentinel-2 GEDI LiDAR ArcGIS Online Experience Builder NDVI AGB Estimation

Calculates NDVI, estimates aboveground biomass (AGB = NDVI × 150), and derives carbon stock values (Carbon Stock = AGB × 0.47) using GDA2020 MGA Zone 54. Built using ArcPy automation and published through ArcGIS Online for interactive stakeholder access.

🛰 Satellite Image Reconstruction & Cloud Removal
M.Tech Research · NRSC/ISRO
M.Tech Thesis · NRSC/ISRO Collaboration · Hyderabad

M.Tech thesis research conducted in collaboration with NRSC/ISRO, Hyderabad. Investigated and compared three cloud removal techniques — Brovey Image Fusion, Conditional Algorithm (SQL), and ECDR — across study areas in Vijayawada, Hyderabad, and Visakhapatnam.

Sentinel-2 Landsat-8 ArcGIS ERDAS Imagine ECDR Algorithm Accuracy Assessment
🏆 Best Accuracy: 89% Overall · Kappa 0.86 (ECDR Algorithm)

The ECDR algorithm outperformed both Fusion (84%, κ=0.80) and Conditional (79%, κ=0.73) methods, demonstrating superior cloud pixel replacement through variance-based Cloud Replacement Pixel (CRP) calculation. Supervised classification was applied post-reconstruction with ground truth validation.
